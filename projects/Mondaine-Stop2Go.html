The Stop2Go Mondaine models reproduce the same movement of the famous clock of the Swiss Railways. The seconds hand stops for 2 seconds, before the minute runs out, to start again with the minutes hand with a new lap of 58 seconds. Still today the trains in Switzerland departs on the minute, this is where it was invented the movement of Stop2Go.

I haven't created this skin, I started from a pre-existing skin made by Monochromatope on DeviantArt (see the credits) that had various problems and developed it as it is today.



Versions

This skin is available in two versions, a Base version and a Complete version. The Base version has a Classic watchface and a Dark version of the Classic one. The Complete version is more customizable with an easy settings interface, from the colors of every element of the clock to the language of the skin.
